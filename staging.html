About Us

Coach Ghana is a new venture started to bring the beautiful game to life. A chance to travel, learn and immerse yourself in the local culture in a safe and fun environment. Coach Ghana provides the chance to see the game in its rawest form and to provide not just a footballing experience but a life experience. Coach Ghana's goal is to showcase the true power that football can have in a community and in people's lives.

Coach Ghana welcomes students, graduates, gap year travellers, travel groups, school groups, football coaches of any level and people who have a love for the game. We will take you through what to expect and the step by step process of your journey to Ghana.

What makes Coach Ghana different from other organisations is we are focused solely on one program. And in doing that we take great care in making each and every individual's journey with us memorable. We are not focused solely on numbers of participants but the quality of our program.

We would love to hear from you. If you would like to know more about the Coach Ghana program please get in touch at info@coachghana.co and we will get back to you as soon as we can.

Program Fees

Coach Ghana offers a dedicated service to help make your experience as smooth, enjoyable and memorable as possible.

Program Fees do not include flights, insurance, your visa or your vaccinations. We do, however offer full support and guidance when it comes to the process and any questions or concerns you may have.

Part of your fee will be set aside to invest back into the program and to the local community. Coach Ghana wants to create a lasting legacy in the local community.

Once you are confirmed on the Coach Ghana program you will receive support from health advice, culture initiation and safety. You will receive a welcome booklet detailing what to know and what to expect.

Arriving in Ghana you will be picked up on arrival and enjoy a night in Accra where you will be given an initiation to the programme, before heading along the coast to Elmina the following day. Your accommodation plus breakfast and dinner are also included in your program fee.

You will be given full support during the programme while in Ghana. We will be on hand 24-Hours to provide emergency support. We want to make sure your journey is a memorable one.
