COACH GHANA

Develop your skillset while being a positive influence

Come be a part of this great organization

Coach Ghana helps connect travelers to local Ghanaian soccer teams with the best players from across Ghana. Come train with some great players and create a difference in the local community. Read Coach Ghana's Plan to Help Ghana.

Experience the game, experience Ghana
COACH. DREAM. INSPIRE

Coach Ghana is a unique social enterprise which offers travellers across the world to live their love of the game and develop their skillset while being a positive influence on the local community.

IMMERSE. THRIVE

Each Coach will have the exciting opportunity to throw themselves into the Ghanaian way of life and to better understand the local culture.

MEMORIES. FOREVER

You will be staying in your own piece of paradise. Fantastic facilities and a great atmosphere. You will never want to leave!
